This egg fried rice is quick and easy to make, perfect for a quick meal or for using leftover rice and ingredients. Follow these simple steps to create a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ sliced green beans or peas
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
